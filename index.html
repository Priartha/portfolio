The Embarrassing Walk Back To Wuyuan:

The Age of Kings has ended. The kingdoms burn and the old gods are silent. The Shadow has stretched its fingers across the land, and in its cold grip, hope dies. In this final, starless night, the people remember the legends,the stories of a time when giants walked and heroes were born of divine and mortal blood. But the stories, they say, are all that is left.

Yet some tales are not forgotten. Some promises are etched not in stone, but in the very heart of the world. And from the earth, and fire, and memory of the lost, comes a reminder of what it means to be an heir to the sun.
